To comply with accessibility standards all img attributes must have alternat text that describes what is being shown

You must have audio tracks that provide equivalent information to video content. You must also provide captions for audio content. Sign language interpretation should be provided for all prerecorded audio.

Information, structure and relationships conveyed through presentation will be available in text. When things are in sequence you should be conveying that. Do not rely solely on sensory characteristics like shape, color, size, visual location, orientation, or sound.

Color should not be used as the only visual means of conveying information. You must have a method to stop or pause audio that plays for more than 3 seconds and you must be able to control audio volume independently. You must have a contrast ratio of at least 4.5:1. You must be able to resize text up to 200% without loss of content or functioinality.
